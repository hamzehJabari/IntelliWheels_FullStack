## RECITALS

WHEREAS, the Founders wish to establish a business venture known as "IntelliWheels" (the "Company"), an AI-powered automotive marketplace;

WHEREAS, the Founders desire to set forth their respective rights, duties, and obligations with respect to the Company;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements set forth herein, and for other good and valuable consideration, the Founders agree as follows:

---

## ARTICLE 1: COMPANY INFORMATION

### 1.1 Company Name
The business shall operate under the name "IntelliWheels" or such other name as the Founders may unanimously agree upon.

### 1.2 Domain
The Company's primary domain shall be: **intelliwheels.co**

### 1.3 Principal Place of Business
Amman, Jordan (or such other location as the Founders may agree)

### 1.4 Purpose
The Company is formed for the purpose of operating an AI-powered automotive marketplace serving Jordan and the GCC region.

### 1.5 Initial Registration
The Company shall initially be registered as a مؤسسة فردية (Sole Proprietorship) under one Founder's name for practical purposes, with this Agreement governing the true ownership structure. The Founders agree to convert to an LLC (شركة ذات مسؤولية محدودة) when revenue exceeds 10,000 JOD per month or within 12 months, whichever comes first.

---

## ARTICLE 2: EQUITY OWNERSHIP

### 2.1 Initial Equity Split
The ownership of the Company shall be divided as follows:

| Founder | Role | Equity |
|---------|------|--------|
| Founder 1 | CEO / Product Lead | 33.33% |
| Founder 2 | CTO / Tech Lead | 33.33% |
| Founder 3 | COO / Business Lead | 33.34% |
| **Total** | | **100.00%** |

### 2.2 Vesting Schedule
All Founder equity shall be subject to a four (4) year vesting schedule with a one (1) year cliff:

- **Year 0-1 (Cliff Period):** No equity vests until the first anniversary
- **At 1 Year:** 25% of equity vests (8.33% of total)
- **Years 2-4:** Remaining 75% vests monthly over 36 months

If a Founder leaves before the cliff:
- Voluntary departure: Forfeits all equity
- Involuntary removal (for cause): Forfeits all equity
- Death or disability: Cliff waived, immediate vesting of 25%

### 2.3 No Dilution Without Consent
No Founder's equity may be diluted without unanimous written consent of all Founders.

---

## ARTICLE 3: ROLES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

### 3.1 Founder Roles

**Founder 1 - CEO / Product Lead:**
- Overall company vision and strategy
- Product direction and prioritization
- Investor and partner relations (if applicable)
- Public representation of the Company
- Final decision authority on product features

**Founder 2 - CTO / Tech Lead:**
- Technical architecture and infrastructure
- Software development and code quality
- AI/ML features and implementation
- Security and data protection
- Technical hiring decisions

**Founder 3 - COO / Business Lead:**
- Business operations and processes
- Dealer acquisition and relations
- Marketing and customer acquisition
- Customer support management
- Financial operations and bookkeeping

### 3.2 Time Commitment
Each Founder agrees to dedicate:
- **Months 1-3:** Minimum 20 hours per week (part-time until graduation)
- **Post-Graduation:** Full-time commitment (40+ hours per week)

### 3.3 Outside Activities
Founders shall not engage in any business activity that directly competes with the Company without unanimous written consent of all Founders.

---

## ARTICLE 4: DECISION MAKING

### 4.1 Day-to-Day Decisions
Each Founder has authority to make decisions within their designated role without requiring approval from other Founders.

### 4.2 Major Decisions (2/3 Vote Required)
The following decisions require approval of at least two (2) Founders:
- Expenditures exceeding 500 JOD
- Hiring employees or contractors
- Entering contracts exceeding 1,000 JOD
- Marketing campaigns exceeding 200 JOD
- Pricing changes
- New feature prioritization

### 4.3 Critical Decisions (Unanimous Required)
The following decisions require unanimous agreement of all three (3) Founders:
- Any change to equity distribution
- Admitting new partners or investors
- Selling or merging the Company
- Taking on debt exceeding 5,000 JOD
- Dissolving the Company
- Changing the Company name
- Removing a Founder

### 4.4 Deadlock Resolution
If Founders cannot reach agreement on a major decision after good faith discussion:
1. The matter shall be tabled for 7 days
2. If still unresolved, an outside mediator may be engaged
3. If still unresolved after mediation, the CEO shall have tie-breaking authority

---

## ARTICLE 5: FINANCIAL MATTERS

### 5.1 Initial Contributions
Each Founder agrees to contribute equally to initial startup costs:
- Estimated initial investment: 500 JOD per Founder (1,500 JOD total)
- Contributions shall be tracked and documented

### 5.2 Founder Compensation
Founders shall receive compensation based on Company revenue:

| Monthly Revenue | Founder Salary (Each) |
|-----------------|----------------------|
| 0 - 3,000 JOD | 0 JOD |
| 3,000 - 5,000 JOD | 300 JOD |
| 5,000 - 10,000 JOD | 500 JOD |
| 10,000 - 20,000 JOD | 800 JOD |
| 20,000+ JOD | To be determined by 2/3 vote |

### 5.3 Profit Distribution
After payment of:
1. All business expenses
2. Founder salaries
3. Tax obligations
4. Reasonable reserves (minimum 3 months operating expenses)

Remaining profits shall be distributed to Founders according to their equity percentages quarterly, or reinvested in the business by 2/3 vote.

### 5.4 Financial Records
- The COO shall maintain accurate financial records
- Monthly financial reports shall be shared with all Founders
- All Founders shall have access to bank accounts and financial systems

## ARTICLE 6: INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

### 6.1 Assignment of IP
All intellectual property created by any Founder for the Company, including but not limited to:
- Source code
- Designs and graphics
- Business processes
- Trademarks and branding
- Customer lists and data

Shall be and remain the exclusive property of the Company.

### 6.2 Prior IP
Any intellectual property created prior to this Agreement and contributed to the Company shall be documented in Exhibit A.

### 6.3 Post-Departure IP
Founders may not use Company IP after departure for any competing purpose.

---

## ARTICLE 7: CONFIDENTIALITY

### 7.1 Confidential Information
Each Founder agrees to keep confidential all non-public information about the Company, including:
- Financial information
- Business strategies
- Customer data
- Technical implementations
- Partner agreements

### 7.2 Duration
Confidentiality obligations shall survive for three (3) years after a Founder's departure.

---

## ARTICLE 8: DEPARTURE OF A FOUNDER

### 8.1 Voluntary Departure
If a Founder voluntarily leaves the Company:
- Before cliff (Year 1): Forfeits all equity
- After cliff: Retains only vested equity
- Unvested equity returns to Company for redistribution

### 8.2 Involuntary Removal (For Cause)
A Founder may be removed by unanimous vote of the other Founders for:
- Material breach of this Agreement
- Fraud or dishonesty
- Conviction of a crime
- Gross negligence
- Failure to perform duties for 30+ consecutive days without valid reason

Removed Founder forfeits all unvested equity.

### 8.3 Buyout Option
Remaining Founders shall have the right (but not obligation) to purchase departing Founder's vested equity at fair market value, payable over 24 months.

### 8.4 Non-Compete
A departing Founder agrees not to:
- Start or join a competing business for 12 months
- Solicit Company employees for 12 months
- Solicit Company customers or dealers for 12 months

---

## ARTICLE 9: DISPUTE RESOLUTION

### 9.1 Good Faith Negotiation
Founders shall first attempt to resolve any dispute through good faith negotiation.

### 9.2 Mediation
If negotiation fails, disputes shall be submitted to mediation before a neutral mediator agreed upon by the Founders.

### 9.3 Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan.

---

## ARTICLE 10: GENERAL PROVISIONS

### 10.1 Entire Agreement
This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Founders regarding the subject matter hereof.

### 10.2 Amendments
This Agreement may only be amended by written instrument signed by all Founders.

### 10.3 Severability
If any provision is found invalid, the remaining provisions shall continue in effect.

### 10.4 Counterparts
This Agreement may be signed in counterparts, each of which shall be deemed an original.
