## 1. Definitions

**"Dealer"** - A verified automotive business registered on IntelliWheels.

**"Listing"** - A vehicle advertisement posted on the Platform.

**"Platform"** - The IntelliWheels website and associated services.

**"Subscription"** - A paid plan providing access to dealer features.

**"Verified Badge"** - A designation indicating verified dealer status.

## 2. Eligibility Requirements

To become a verified dealer, you must:

### 2.1 Business Requirements
- [ ] Hold a valid commercial registration in Jordan
- [ ] Operate a legitimate automotive business
- [ ] Have a physical business location
- [ ] Possess required business licenses

### 2.2 Documentation Required
- Commercial registration certificate
- Tax registration number
- Business license
- Proof of business address
- Owner/representative identification
- Bank account details (for any payouts)

### 2.3 Verification Process
1. Submit dealer application with required documents
2. Platform reviews application (within 5 business days)
3. Physical verification may be conducted
4. Approval or rejection notification
5. Upon approval, dealer dashboard access granted

## 3. Dealer Obligations

### 3.1 Listing Accuracy
You agree to:
- Provide accurate vehicle information
- Use genuine photos of actual vehicles
- Update or remove listings promptly when sold
- Disclose known defects or issues
- Honor advertised prices

### 3.2 Business Conduct
You agree to:
- Respond to inquiries within 24-48 hours
- Treat all users professionally and respectfully
- Not engage in discriminatory practices
- Maintain confidentiality of user information
- Comply with all applicable laws

### 3.3 Prohibited Activities
You must NOT:
- Post fraudulent or misleading listings
- Sell stolen vehicles
- Engage in price manipulation
- Spam users or post excessive listings
- Circumvent Platform fees or policies
- Share your dealer credentials
- Collect user data for unauthorized purposes
